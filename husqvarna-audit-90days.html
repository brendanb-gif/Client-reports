Issues Identified
1
PMAX – BRAND – Performance is paused — it was the best-performing campaign in the account High
Over the 90-day window, PMAX – BRAND – Performance generated 319.9 conversions at $40.92/conv with a 12.3x ROAS ($160,839 conversion value on $13,090 spend). That is by far the most efficient campaign in the account. It is currently paused. By comparison, the two active PMax campaigns return 7.85x and 8.72x ROAS. If this was paused as part of a restructure, the replacement campaigns need to match its performance before the brand PMax budget is fully reallocated. The account should either reactivate this campaign or clearly document why it was paused and confirm the current PMax structure compensates for the loss.

4
Search – BRAND – Performance was also paused mid-period — losing 21 conversions at $194/conv High
The "Search – BRAND – Performance" campaign (now paused) generated $4,160 in spend, 1,232 clicks, and 21.4 conversions at $194/conv over the 90-day window. Its "Automower" AG had a 36.1% CTR and $3.06 avg CPC. Combined with the paused PMAX BRAND, the account has effectively removed its two best-performing brand-specific campaigns. The active brand campaign (Search – BRAND – Husqvarna Robotic Mower on Manual CPC) is now carrying all brand search volume.
5
Brand search on Manual CPC while all other search campaigns use Smart Bidding Medium
The active brand campaign (Search – BRAND – Husqvarna Robotic Mower, $17,652 spend, 122 convs) is the only search campaign on Manual CPC. All other search campaigns use Maximise Conversions. At 122 conversions over 90 days there is more than enough data to move to Target CPA or Maximise Conversions. Manual CPC requires ongoing bid management and is likely leaving efficiency on the table at this volume.

Husqvarna Automower Price AG has high avg CPC ($5.07) with low conversion rate (1.13%) Medium
The Husqvarna Automower Price AG spent $1,342 for 265 clicks and only 3 conversions (1.13% CVR) at a $5.07 avg CPC — the highest avg CPC of any active brand ad group. Compare to the Husqvarna Robot Mower AG at $2.83 avg CPC and 2.36% CVR. Price-intent searchers are being sent to a page that doesn’t convert at a rate commensurate with the cost. Review the landing page and ad copy for this ad group, and consider whether price terms should be directed to a clearer pricing/comparison page.

Additionally, "husqvarna automower price australia" (16 clicks, $103, 0 convs) and "husqvarna robot mower price" (30 clicks, $116, 0 convs) are the biggest cost offenders in this AG with no return over 90 days.

What's Working

PMAX – Non Shopping & PMAX – Purchase Performance

Combined $18,750 spend, 322 conversions, $154,119 conversion value, ~8.2x blended ROAS. These are the active PMax workhorses and are operating efficiently.

Husqvarna Robot Mower AG

4,211 clicks at $2.83 avg CPC, 99.2 conversions, 2.36% CVR. The "husqvarna robot mower" search term alone: 1,661 clicks at $1.83 avg CPC, 41.8 conversions. Highest conversion volume of any individual ad group.

Smart Mower & Electric Automower AGs

Smart Mower: 4.19% CVR, 13 conversions. Electric Automower: 4.45% CVR, 11 conversions. Both outperform the main Robot Lawn Mower AG on conversion rate by 2.5x, suggesting stronger purchase intent from these query sets.

Automower Acreage AG

353 clicks, 8 conversions, 2.27% CVR, $4.04 avg CPC. The acreage/large-property angle is consistently converting and is an underexplored audience segment with room to scale.

Buy AG in Buy Intent

939 clicks, 23 conversions, 2.45% CVR. Strongest CVR in the Buy Intent campaign. Despite some non-intent term leakage, the core purchase-intent queries are converting well at $191/conv.

Negative Keyword Library

10 shared lists covering 62 competitors, 803 world locations, 49 model names, 36 other products, and question words. Solid structural foundation that is clearly protecting the account from a large volume of irrelevant traffic — just needs "how" added to close the main gap.
